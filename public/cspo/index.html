About CSPO

The Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) course is a 2-day immersive learning experience. If you’re someone who is comfortable with the “business side” of projects, you are probably the right person to aspire to achieve a CSPO certification. While the Certified ScrumMaster® (CSM®) helps the Scrum team work together to learn and implement Scrum, as a CSPO, you create the product vision, order the product backlog, and make sure the best possible job is completed for the customer.

Certification

  • A Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO) certification
  • A CSPO digital badge to promote their accomplishment online

      • NOTE: The Certified Scrum Trainer determines your completion of the course and has the discretion in awarding certificates based on your engagement in the classroom. Finally, certificates expire two years from the date the certification is earned. Scrum Alliance certification renewal guidelines and fees apply.

About the Instructor

Rick Waters (Certified Scrum Trainer) is a veteran of the US Military. He has an in-depth background in customer service, and software development. Rick’s strengths are focused on Scrum, enterprise Scrum, business agility and Kanban. He has contributed to the success of some of the largest Agile transformations in the Chicagoland area, since 2013. His dedication to service is underscored by his volunteer involvement in educating youths, and helping veterans re-enter the civilian workforce in national non-profit organizations.
