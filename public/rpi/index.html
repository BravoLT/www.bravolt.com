What is a Raspberry Pi?

Despite what the name might infer, it is not a de-licious baked good. The Raspberry Pi is a low cost computer that can fit into the palm of your hand. While the Raspberry Pi might be small, it functions just the same as a full size computer. It originat-ed in the UK where it was designed to bring basic computer science to developing countries. How-ever, it quickly gained popularity among academia and hobbyists alike. What is a raspberry pi?
